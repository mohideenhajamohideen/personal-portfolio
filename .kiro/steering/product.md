# Product Overview

This is a personal portfolio website for Mohideen, an AI Generalist and Cloud Architect. The site showcases professional experience, projects, and technical expertise in AI/ML, cloud architecture, and automation.

## Key Features

- Minimalist, responsive design with dark/light mode toggle
- Project showcase with categorized work (Infrastructure, FinOps, Gen AI, Automation)
- Visitor tracking system with persistent storage
- Professional branding focused on AI and cloud expertise
- Contact form and social media integration

## Target Audience

- Potential clients and employers
- Technical professionals and peers
- Organizations seeking AI/cloud consulting services

## Brand Identity

- Professional yet approachable tone
- Focus on outcomes and business value
- Emphasis on sustainability and ethical AI practices
- Technical expertise balanced with business acumen
